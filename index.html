OUR SERVICE

The Tata Foundation supports various sports centers across India, focusing on excellence and accessibility. These centers provide state-of-the-art facilities and professional coaching to nurture talent in sports such as athletics, hockey, and football. Notable initiatives include the Tata Football Academy in Jamshedpur and the Tata Archery Academy.

TATA STEEL Foundation

TATA STEEL

"Tata Steel Foundation (TSF) is the corporate social responsibility arm of Tata Steel, dedicated to making a meaningful impact on society. We strive to create sustainable development through initiatives in education, healthcare, livelihood, and sports. Our aim is to empower communities by providing opportunities and resources that foster growth and resilience. With a focus on inclusivity and innovation, TSF operates various programs and centers across India, addressing the needs of underprivileged and marginalized populations. By building strong partnerships and leveraging the strength of the Tata Group, we are committed to driving positive change and contributing to the nation's progress."
